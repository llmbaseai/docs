## What is LLM Base?

LLM Base is Europe's leading platform for enterprise-grade AI solutions, seamlessly integrating state-of-the-art inference, branded chat experiences, and bespoke model development to empower your digital transformation. We put privacy first, ensuring your data is always secure and trusted.

### We offer
- **AI Inference:** Access the largest collection of cutting-edge AI models, securely hosted on EU servers and ready to integrate into your software.
- **AI Chat Bots:** Customize your customer experience by powering chatbots with your data and a fully branded UI.
- **AI Web Scrapers:** Effortlessly extract data from any website using our intuitive scraper API. (Coming Soon)
- **AI Agents:** Transform your business processes with intelligent automation designed exclusively for enterprises.
- **AI Model Development:** Benefit from custom, high-precision AI models built on extensive datasets for unparalleled performance.
- **Consulting:** Unsure how AI can revolutionize your business? Our experts are here to guide you.
